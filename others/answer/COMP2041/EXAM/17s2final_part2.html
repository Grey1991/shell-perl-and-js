Answering either of q14 or q15 correctly will meet part of the exam hurdle requirement of answering 2 questions correctly to pass COMP[29]041.

But answering both q14 and q15 correctly does not meet the exam hurdle requirement, you must also answer one of q16-q21 correctly.

Answering either of q16 or q17 correctly meets part of the exam hurdle requirement of answering 2 questions correctly to pass COMP[29]041. But answering both q16 and q17 correctly does not meet the exam hurdle requirement, you must also answer one of q14,q15,q18-q21.

For example, answering q14 and q15 would not alone meet the exam hurdle requirement.

For example, answering q14 and q17 correctly would meet the exam hurdle requirement. and for example, answering q15 and q18 would meet the exam hurdle requirement.

Questions in this part must be entered into separate files and submitted with give.

Make sure you use 'EXACTLY' the specified file name.

You will not be able to enter the answers to questions in this part until after the first 35 minutes.

During the first 35 minutes the only programs you can run in the exam environment are the applications to enter answers to parts 1 and 2 of the exam and view part 3 of the exam clock (xclock), calculator (xcalc) and firefox to access cheatsheets and other documentation.

After the first 35 minutes the exam environment allows you to run a limited set of commands so you can test and debug your answers.

The exam environment logs all your keystrokes. Biometric keystroke timing data will be collected to confirm your identity.

The exam environment does not allow access to files in your CSE account or any other CSE account. It does not allow access to the web or other network access

You may submit your answers as many times as you like. The last submission 'ONLY' will be marked.
